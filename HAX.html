About the Hackathon

AloThon Hackathon 2025 is a 24-hour invention marathon where students from across the country will come together to build, code, and innovate. This year's theme is "AI and IOT combination for a Smarter World."

Build

Create a web or mobile application, a hardware project, or a creative solution that addresses a core problem.

Learn

Attend workshops led by industry experts and get mentorship to level up your skills.

Connect

Network with peers, meet recruiters, and connect with top tech companies.

Accommodation for Outsiders

To ensure a comfortable experience for out-of-town participants, we have arranged for the following accommodation options.

On-Campus Hostel

We offer free, shared hostel rooms for the duration of the hackathon (Nov 15-16). Rooms will be allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Please indicate your need for accommodation in the registration form.

*Separate facilities for male and female students will be provided.*

Nearby Hotels

For those who prefer private accommodation, we have partnered with local hotels to provide special discounted rates. Mention "AloThon" when booking.

  • The Grand Inn (2km away)
  • Tech Hub Inn (1km away)
  • Campus View Hotel (3km away)
